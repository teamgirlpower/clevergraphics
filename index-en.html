Clever Graphics, tutorials for free online software

We are a Finnish company in graphic business and we have started our business in 2005. Our specialities are free graphic designing softwares that you can download online. We sell tutorials for new and more advanced users so that they can get everything out of the software. We are continuously working with the developers of the free softwares to keep up with the new trends and ways.

Currently we have 20 graphic design personnel in our service in Finland and abroad.

Even though we sell tutorials for free softwares, their usage is quite limited in business world and for that reason we don't use them. In our company we use Adobe Photoshop and InDesign for graphics, Trello in our scheduling and taskhandling and our website has been created with Google Web Designer.
